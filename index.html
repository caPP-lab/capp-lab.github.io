Research

We develop AI methods that create real-world impact for people and the planet.

Our research sits at the intersection of machine learning, optimization, and causal inference — applied to planetary health with a focus on biodiversity conservation and equitable resource allocation.
🦁

Wildlife Conservation & Anti-Poaching

Predictive models and patrol planning algorithms deployed with rangers in the field. Our PAWS system uses online learning to forecast illegal activity and enable more effective conservation management.

🎰

Restless Bandits & Sequential Decisions

Algorithms for restless multi-armed bandit problems in resource-constrained settings — from ranger patrol planning to maternal health — where limited interventions must be allocated across many beneficiaries over time.

🔗

Causal Inference for Conservation

Causal methods to evaluate conservation interventions from observational data, enabling rigorous evidence-based policy under the confounding challenges in ecological and social systems.

⚖️

Equity & Access in Algorithms

Co-directing EAAMO, we advance mechanism design, fair optimization, and algorithmic auditing to improve access to opportunity for historically marginalized communities.

🌍

Participatory AI & Data Justice

Designing AI systems that incorporate local knowledge, support contestability, and respect community rights in natural resource management and conservation decision-making.

🌡️

Climate & Planetary Health

Applying AI and operations research to planetary health — climate adaptation, land use, biodiversity monitoring — in partnership with Climate Change AI and others.
