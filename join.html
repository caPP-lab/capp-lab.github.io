Express interest

The caPP Lab at Columbia University always looking for motivated members who are interested in machine learning, online learning, reinforcement learning, and AI for biodiversity.

If you're interested in research opportunities, please fill out this interest form.

The form will ask for a PDF of your resume/CV and transcripts, your interests, and any prior experience that may be relevant.

Your responses will help us learn more about your background and interests so we can match you with relevant opportunities and collaborations. Please note that there are not always opportunities available, and we may not be able to respond individually to every inquiry. However, when an opportunity to join a project does arise, we will first look through respondents on the interest form to identify potential candidates.

We look forward to hearing from you!

Prospective PhD students

If you're applying to Columbia for a PhD program, please mention Lily Xu as a faculty of interest in your PhD application. Feel free to send an email expressing your interest as well, but note that I am not always able to respond individually to every inquiry.

Students with a strong background in applied math, optimization, or operations research can apply to Columbia IEOR. Students with a background in computer science can apply to Columbia CS. Students with a background in ecology, economics, or other disciplines can apply to Columbia E3B or the Sustainable Development PhD program at Columbia SIPA, where I may also be able to serve as a co-advisor.

No need to fill out the interest form unless you're interested in potentially working on projects beforehand.
